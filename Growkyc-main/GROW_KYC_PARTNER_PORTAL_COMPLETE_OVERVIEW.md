## 📱 **KEY WORKFLOWS**

### **1. New Client Onboarding (Individual)**

**Steps:**
1. Select "Individual" entity type
2. Enter basic details (name, DOB, address)
3. Upload ID documents (100-point check)
4. Verify identity via InfoTrack
5. PEP screening via World-Check
6. Adverse media check
7. Risk assessment (auto-scored)
8. Source of wealth declaration
9. Final review by Compliance Officer
10. Partner approval (if high risk)
11. Client activated

**Timeline:** 15-30 minutes (low risk), 2-5 days (high risk)

---

### **2. High Risk Override Approval (Partner)**

**Scenario:** Previous bankrupt client wants to re-engage

**Steps:**
1. Analyst flags client as high risk
2. Analyst completes EDD (Enhanced Due Diligence)
3. Analyst prepares justification memo
4. Case escalated to Partner Dashboard
5. Partner reviews:
   - Historical records
   - Current financial position
   - Bankruptcy discharge date
   - New source of wealth
   - Risk mitigation plan
6. Partner decision:
   - ✅ Approve with conditions
   - ❌ Reject
   - 📝 Request more info
7. System logs decision + justification
8. Automatic notification to analyst
9. If approved: Client risk tier adjusted
10. Ongoing monitoring activated

---

### **3. Fund Investor KYC Refresh**

**Scenario:** Annual KYC refresh for fund investors

**Automated Process:**
1. System identifies investors due for refresh (annual)
2. Automatic email sent to investor
3. Secure portal link for document upload
4. Investor uploads updated documents
5. System OCR extracts data
6. Auto-validation against previous records
7. Compliance Officer reviews changes
8. Partner approval (if material changes)
9. Fund compliance status updated
10. ASIC reporting (if required)

**SLA:** 30 days before KYC expiry

---

### **4. Transaction Monitoring Alert**

**Scenario:** Suspicious cash deposits detected

**Steps:**
1. Transaction monitoring engine detects pattern
2. Alert generated (Medium/High/Critical)
3. Analyst receives notification
4. Analyst opens case
5. Analyst reviews:
   - Transaction history (6 months)
   - Client profile
   - Source of funds
   - Business activity justification
6. Analyst conducts enhanced review
7. If suspicious: Prepare SMR (Suspicious Matter Report)
8. Partner reviews SMR
9. Partner approves filing to AUSTRAC
10. System submits SMR electronically
11. AUSTRAC confirmation received
12. Case closed with full audit trail

**Timeline:** 24-72 hours from alert to AUSTRAC filing
