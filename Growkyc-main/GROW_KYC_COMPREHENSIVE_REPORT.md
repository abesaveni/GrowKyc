# 📊 GROW KYC MODULE - COMPREHENSIVE REPORT

## 🔷 **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

**Grow KYC** is a Regulatory Operating System designed for **AML/CTF, ACL & AFSL Compliance** in Australia. It provides a complete solution for accounting firms, lenders, fund managers, and financial services organizations to meet their regulatory obligations under AUSTRAC, NCCP, and ASIC requirements.

**Status:** ✅ **Production Ready**  
**Primary Users:** Compliance Officers, Partners, Auditors, AML Analysts  
**Regulatory Coverage:** AUSTRAC Tranche 2, NCCP Responsible Lending, ASIC Reportable Situations  
**Compliance Date:** Ready for AUSTRAC Tranche 2 (1 July 2026)

---

## 🎯 **PRIMARY PURPOSE**

Grow KYC is the **compliance backbone** of the Grow Platform. It enables organizations to:

1. **Verify Client Identity** - Digital identity verification and document checks
2. **Screen for Risk** - Sanctions, PEP, adverse media, insolvency screening
3. **Manage Compliance Cases** - Customer Due Diligence (CDD) and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
4. **Monitor Ongoing Risk** - Continuous monitoring of client risk profiles
5. **Track Regulatory Obligations** - Countdown clocks for regulatory deadlines
6. **Store Evidence** - Immutable audit trail and evidence vault
7. **Report to Regulators** - AUSTRAC, ASIC, and other regulatory reporting
8. **Integrate Data Providers** - Connect to Trulioo, InfoTrack, GreenID, WorldCheck

## 🔄 **TYPICAL WORKFLOWS**

### **Workflow 1: New Client Onboarding**
1. Client enters information via portal
2. System performs ID verification (Trulioo/GreenID)
3. Sanctions and PEP screening executed
4. Risk rating calculated
5. CDD case created
6. Evidence stored in vault
7. Approval workflow (if high risk)
8. Client approved or restricted
9. Monitoring subscription activated
10. Regulatory clock started

---

### **Workflow 2: Sanctions Hit Investigation**
1. Monitoring alert triggered
2. Auto-restriction applied
3. Case created automatically
4. Assigned to AML analyst
5. Analyst reviews match details
6. Investigates false positive
7. Documents findings
8. Compliance officer reviews
9. Override decision (if appropriate)
10. Regulatory filing (if required)

---

### **Workflow 3: Annual CDD Refresh**
1. Regulatory clock triggers alert
2. CDD refresh case created
3. Client contacted for updated information
4. New verification performed
5. Re-screening executed
6. Risk rating reassessed
7. Updated evidence stored
8. Case closed
9. New 2-year clock started

---

### **Workflow 4: Suspicious Matter Reporting**
1. Analyst identifies suspicious activity
2. SMR case created
3. Evidence collected
4. Risk assessment performed
5. Management review
6. Compliance officer approval
7. 24-hour clock tracking
8. AUSTRAC Online submission
9. Case closed with evidence
10. 7-year retention applied

---

### **Workflow 5: ASIC Reportable Situation**
1. Breach identified
2. Significance assessment
3. Reportable situation case created
4. Investigation commenced
5. Impact analysis
6. Remediation plan
7. 30-day reporting clock
8. ASIC submission
9. Ongoing monitoring
10. Final report and closure
